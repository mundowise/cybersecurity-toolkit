# Ethical Use Policy

## Purpose

This software is developed for legitimate network security research, education, and authorized WiFi and network scanning purposes. It is designed to help security professionals audit networks they are authorized to assess.

## Acceptable Use

- Network audits with **written authorization** from the network owner
- Educational purposes in controlled lab environments
- Personal learning on networks you own and operate
- Authorized penetration testing engagements
- Identifying unauthorized devices on your own network
- CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions
- Academic and research use in isolated environments

## Unacceptable Use

- Scanning networks you do not own or have explicit authorization to scan
- Intercepting wireless traffic without the consent of all parties
- Using scan results to target or exploit third-party systems
- Mapping networks for malicious purposes such as planning unauthorized access
- Any activity that violates local, state, federal, or international law

## Legal Framework

Unauthorized network scanning and wireless interception are illegal in most jurisdictions. Users must comply with all applicable laws, including but not limited to:

- **United States**: Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA), 18 U.S.C. § 1030; Electronic Communications Privacy Act (ECPA)
- **European Union**: Directive 2013/40/EU on attacks against information systems; GDPR (where personal data is captured)
- **United Kingdom**: Computer Misuse Act 1990; Wireless Telegraphy Act 2006
- **Canada**: Criminal Code Section 342.1
- **Australia**: Criminal Code Act 1995, Part 10.7; Radiocommunications Act 1992

Even passive WiFi scanning that captures network identifiers (BSSIDs, SSIDs) may be subject to data protection regulations in certain jurisdictions.

## Responsibility

The developers of this software assume **no liability** for misuse. Users are solely responsible for ensuring their use of this tool complies with all applicable laws and regulations. Always obtain written authorization before scanning any network you do not personally own.

## Reporting Misuse

If you become aware of this tool being used for unauthorized or malicious purposes, please report it to the maintainers immediately via the contact information in SECURITY.md.
